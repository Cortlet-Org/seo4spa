# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the **seo4spa** project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- age
- gender identity and expression
- sexual orientation
- disability
- physical appearance
- race
- ethnicity
- nationality
- religion
- level of experience
- personal background

We are committed to building a welcoming, supportive, and collaborative open-source community.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of **positive behavior** that we encourage:

- Being kind, respectful, and constructive
- Providing helpful and actionable feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others
- Welcoming new contributors
- Helping others understand the project and codebase
- Keeping discussions professional and friendly

### Examples of **unacceptable behavior** include:

- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind (public or private)
- Publishing private information without permission
- Trolling or intentionally provoking others
- Disrespecting boundaries or ignoring requests to stop
- Disruptive behavior that harms community health

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Reviewing contributions fairly
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct when necessary
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior

Maintainers have the right to:

- Remove or edit comments
- Reject or revert contributions
- Ban contributors for serious or repeated violations

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- The repository
- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Public community spaces related to the project
- Any direct communication with maintainers
